Learning Path

How to Progress

Follow this sequence and you'll go from zero to playing complete Bollywood songs — with genuine musical understanding, not just muscle memory.

🎸
Tune Up
Standard tuning: E A D G B e. Use a clip-on tuner or a free phone app. Every lesson assumes standard tuning — don't skip this step.
🤚
Open Chords
Learn Am, G, E, E7, C, and D. These six chords cover the majority of Bollywood's 1970s–90s golden era. Visit the Chord Library.
🎵
First Melody
Start with Chura Liya Hai Tumne. It uses only three strings, four patterns, and teaches string navigation, slides, and phrasing all at once.
👂
Ear Training
Use the ▶ Play buttons on every section tab. Listen first, then play. Your ear is the most important instrument — train it alongside your fingers.
📊
Music Theory
Understanding why Am → E7 creates tension makes the music make sense. Read the theory section in the Chord Library after your first song.
🔁
Repetition
Practice each phrase 20 times before moving on. Speed comes from smoothness, not effort. Slow and clean beats fast and sloppy every time.
🎤
Sing Along
Hum the lyrics as you play. Your voice locks in the timing better than a metronome. Rafi sahab will guide your fingers if you let him.
🚀
Barre Chords
Once open chords feel automatic, learn F major barre. It unlocks every key on the neck. Difficult at first — entirely worth the effort.
