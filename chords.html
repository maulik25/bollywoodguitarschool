Music Theory

Understanding Chords

Before memorising shapes, understanding why chords sound the way they do makes learning far faster. Here are the core concepts every Bollywood guitarist needs.

🎵
Major Chords
Built from the root, major 3rd, and perfect 5th of a scale. They sound bright, resolved, and happy. Formula: 135. In Bollywood: G major anchors the joyful verses of Chura Liya.
🌙
Minor Chords
The 3rd is flattened by one semitone. Formula: 1♭35. They sound emotional, longing, melancholic — perfect for Bollywood's romantic minor keys. Am is the home chord of Chura Liya.
🎸
Dominant 7th
A major chord with a flattened 7th added: 135♭7. Creates tension that wants to resolve. E7 in Chura Liya pulls strongly back to Am — that tension is the song's emotional engine.
🤚
Barre Chords
One finger (usually index) presses all 6 strings across a single fret, acting as a movable nut. The chord shape stays the same — only the fret position changes. F major is the classic beginner barre challenge.
🇮🇳
Bollywood Keys
Most Bollywood songs are written in Am, D minor, or G major. The Am → G → E7 → Am progression appears in dozens of 1970s–80s hits. Mastering these four chords opens up a huge repertoire instantly.
🔄
Chord Progressions
Chura Liya: AmGAmE. Repeats throughout the mukhda. The i–VII–i–V progression is one of the most common in Hindi film music — romantic, cyclical, yearning.
Rhythm

Strumming Patterns

The right strumming pattern transforms a chord sequence into a song. These are the patterns used in the songs on this site.

⬇️⬆️
DUUDUD
Down · Up · Up · Down · Up · Down. The classic Bollywood strumming pattern — used in Chura Liya and hundreds of other 70s–80s songs. Start at 60 BPM and build up to 110 BPM.
⬇️⬇️
DDUD
Down · Down · Up · Down. A simpler 4-beat pattern for beginners. Gives a stronger rhythmic pulse and is easier to keep steady while switching chords.
🤌
Fingerpick Arpeggio
Thumb plays bass string · Index plays G · Middle plays B · Ring plays e. Used for slow, intimate Bollywood ballads. The tab lessons on this site use this approach for melody playing.
